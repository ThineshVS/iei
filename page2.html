XIONA Medical Assistant

First ever robot designed and constructed for the screening of corona.

Xiona robot helps doctors to avoid direct contact with the screening patients and it does the preliminary checking for the
patients and visitorsand it involves various safety measures which is to be followed to make the environment healthier.
Xiona is a completely automated robot and the accuracy of each step followed by the robot is above 90%.
It is well trained with enormous real life datasets and will be considered as important asset for medical advancement.
